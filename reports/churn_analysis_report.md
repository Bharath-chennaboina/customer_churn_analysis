## 1. Executive Summary

Customer churn — the rate at which customers stop doing business with a company — is one of the most important metrics for subscription businesses. This analysis identifies customers at high risk of churning, uncovers the root drivers, and prescribes data-driven retention actions.

**Key Finding:** The overall churn rate is **26.5%**, meaning roughly 1 in 4 customers leaves within a given period. Month-to-month contract customers represent the highest churn risk at **42.7%**.

## 3. Key Drivers of Churn

### 3.1 Contract Type (Strongest Driver)
| Contract | Churn Rate |
|---|---|
| Month-to-Month | **42.7%** |
| One Year | 9.3% |
| Two Year | 2.8% |

Customers on month-to-month contracts churn **15x more** than two-year customers.

### 3.2 Tenure
Customers in their first 6 months have a churn rate of ~50%. After 2 years, churn drops below 10%.

### 3.3 Internet Service Type
| Service | Churn Rate |
|---|---|
| Fiber Optic | 41.9% |
| DSL | 19.0% |
| No Internet | 7.4% |

Fiber optic customers churn more — likely due to higher prices and competition.

### 3.4 Technical Support
Customers without tech support churn at **41.7%** vs **15.2%** for those with it.

### 3.5 Payment Method
Electronic check payers churn at **45.3%** — highest among all payment methods.

## 7. Retention Strategy Recommendations

### 🔴 High Risk Customers
- **Personal outreach** via phone within 24–48 hours
- **Discount incentive**: 15–20% off for switching to a 1-year or 2-year contract
- **Assign dedicated account manager** for top-CLV customers
- **Escalate unresolved technical issues** immediately

### 🟡 Medium Risk Customers
- **Proactive check-in** email or call within 7 days
- **Bundle upgrade offer**: Add streaming or security at no extra cost for 3 months
- **Loyalty reward program**: Points, credits, or anniversary gifts
- **Personalized savings report**: Show how much they save vs competitors

### 🟢 Low Risk Customers
- **Regular engagement**: Monthly newsletter with product updates
- **Upsell**: Streaming, device protection, or multi-line discounts
- **Referral program**: Incentivize satisfied customers to bring others
- **NPS survey**: Gather feedback to maintain satisfaction

## 8. Business Impact Estimate
